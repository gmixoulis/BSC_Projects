Experience the roots of Heartcore’s Dynamic Pilates, developing confidence with our approach and unique style of CoreFormer™ training. In this workshop you'll learn to better understand the mind body principles of Pilates and how we fuse them through the foundational exercises of our Signature class. Guiding you to recognise the correct posture in your body through a series of strengthening and opening movements, with the time to experience and really feel the postural benefit in correct muscle recruitment. Learn how to modify positions, enhance mobility and techniques to connect deeply to your core. We hope to see some ‘a-ha’ moments before the 90 minutes is out, and a warmth to your core, just so you remember where it is for your next class!
